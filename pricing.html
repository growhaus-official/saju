Common questions
Is this a subscription?
No. Every purchase is a one-time charge. You pay once and the reading is yours permanently — no recurring charges, ever.
What payment methods are accepted?
All major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), Apple Pay, Google Pay, and PayPal.
Can I get a refund?
Refunds are available within 7 days only if a technical error prevented you from accessing your reading — proof via screenshot is required. Once the paid content has been accessed, refunds are not available. See our refund policy for full details.
How is my reading generated?
Your birth data is used to calculate your Four Pillars chart using traditional Korean Saju methodology. The interpretation is generated by AI (Llama 3.3 70B via Groq) and delivered instantly. All calculations happen in your browser — your data is never stored on our servers.
Is Saju the same as Western astrology?
No. Saju (Korean Four Pillars) is based on the exact year, month, day, and hour of birth mapped to a 60-year cycle of Heavenly Stems and Earthly Branches. It's a distinct tradition from Western sun-sign astrology.

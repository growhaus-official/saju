2. How Your Birth Data Is Processed

When you enter your birth details, all Four Pillars calculations happen entirely within your web browser using JavaScript. This data is transmitted to our AI provider (Groq) only to generate the text of your reading — it is not stored by Groq beyond the duration of a single API request, per their data handling policy.

We do not have a database of users' birth data. If you close your browser tab, your data is gone.

3. AI-Generated Content

Your reading text is generated by Llama 3.3 70B running via Groq's API. The prompt sent to Groq includes your birth date, birth time, birth city, gender, and focus area. No name is included in the AI prompt. Groq's privacy policy applies to this processing: groq.com/privacy-policy

4. Payment Processing

All payments are processed by Paddle.com, our Merchant of Record. When you make a purchase, you are transacting directly with Paddle. We receive only a notification that a payment was completed — we do not receive or store your card number, bank details, or full billing address.

Paddle's privacy policy: paddle.com/legal/privacy

5. Cookies & Analytics

We use minimal, privacy-respecting analytics to understand how many people visit the site and which features are used. This data is aggregated and anonymous — we cannot identify individual users from it.

We do not use advertising cookies or tracking pixels.

6. Third-Party Services We Use

  • Groq — AI inference (reading generation)
  • Paddle — Payment processing
  • Vercel — Website hosting
  • Google Fonts — Typography (may log your IP per Google's policy)

7. Your Rights

Because we do not store personal data about users without account creation, most traditional data rights (access, deletion, portability) are satisfied by default — there is simply no personal data stored to retrieve or delete.

If you have made a purchase and wish to have your email removed from Paddle's records, please contact Paddle directly or email us at growhaus.official@gmail.com and we will assist.

8. Children's Privacy

SAJU is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has used the service, please contact us.

9. Data Security

All data in transit between your browser and our servers (and to Groq) is encrypted via TLS 1.3. Since we store minimal personal data, the attack surface for data breaches is extremely limited.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the service evolves. Material changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.

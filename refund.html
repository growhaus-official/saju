Refund Policy
Last updated: March 2025

The short version: Once you've viewed your paid reading, it's been delivered — no refund. If a technical error stopped you from accessing it at all, send us a screenshot within 7 days and we'll refund you in full.

Our Guarantee

How to Request a Refund

1
Email us at growhaus.official@gmail.com with the subject line "Refund Request"
2
Include your purchase email address, purchase date (from your Paddle receipt), and a screenshot or screen recording showing the technical issue
3
We'll confirm and process the refund within 3 business days
4
Refunds appear back on your original payment method within 5–10 business days, depending on your bank

Eligibility

Refunds are available if:

  • Your request is made within 7 days of the original purchase date
  • You purchased directly through SAJU (via Paddle checkout)

Non-Refundable Cases

No refund once content is accessed. SAJU readings are generated and delivered as digital content. Once the paid sections have been unlocked and viewed, the content is considered consumed and delivered in full. Pursuant to the EU Digital Content Directive, Article 16(m), the right of withdrawal does not apply to digital content where delivery has begun with the consumer's prior express consent and acknowledgement that the right of withdrawal is thereby lost. By proceeding with payment, you acknowledge and agree that your right of withdrawal is forfeited upon accessing the paid content.

Refunds will not be issued in the following circumstances:

  • Content accessed: The paid reading has been unlocked and viewed, even partially
  • Change of mind: Dissatisfaction with the reading's interpretation or style is not grounds for a refund, as the content was delivered as described
  • Time limit exceeded: The request is made more than 7 days after purchase
  • No proof provided: The refund request does not include a screenshot or screen recording demonstrating a technical failure
  • Policy abuse: Repeated refund requests across the same or similar products
  • Third-party purchase: The purchase was made through a platform not operated by SAJU

Technical Issues & How to Prove Them

If a technical failure on our end prevented you from accessing your reading (e.g. blank screen after payment, AI generation error, page crash before content loaded), you are entitled to a full refund provided:

  • You submit your request within 7 days of purchase
  • You attach a screenshot or screen recording that clearly shows: (a) your purchase confirmation, and (b) the error or inaccessible state of the reading

We review all technical refund requests individually and aim to respond within 2 business days. We will not issue a refund based on a written description alone — visual proof is required to prevent abuse of this policy.
